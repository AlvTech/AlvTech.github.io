Älv Project is a curated retail platform that creates innovative conceptual spaces purveying womenswear, menswear, jewellery and accessories.

Our concept stems from a view of retail as an expression of art, experimental in nature and ever evolving with the times.

With the vision of building intercultural communication and a purpose to initiate intercountry dialogue, we conceive informative pop-up galleries in various cities to exhibit visionary designers portraying distinctive cultural characteristics.

Laid out in discerning settings we organise vis-à-vis with trade experts, concocting enagaging milieu with fine brands that illustrate ingenuity in design and dexterity in craft.

We aim to promote multiculturalism. Fashion and design as tools of cultural exchange bring the world closer by promoting sociocultural sharing and understanding, which is what Älv Project is set to do.

Through fostering industry collaborations, we form cross-cultural relationships between designers from distinct native or artistic backgrounds to create a métissage.

The intent is to produce artistic concoctions that reach and attain sophisticated levels of aesthetics, delivering exciting new designs to the market.
